Anthurium

Anthurium andraeanum, commonly called flamingo lily or painter's palette, is native to Colombia and Ecuador. It is an epiphytic evergreen tropical perennial of the Arum family that is most noted for its attractive waxy, palette-shaped, bright red spathes and contrasting dark green foliage.

Best conditions
  • insolation : Bright diffused light
  • room temperature : 18 - 20 °C
  • room humidity : 50% - 70%
  • soil moisture : 50% - 70%
  • watering frequency : Twice a week
Tips and Tricks

  • Grow your anthurium in a spot that gets plenty of bright, indirect light but no direct sunshine.
  • High humidity is best, so a bathroom or conservatory is ideal for them.
  • Grouping plants together can help to raise humidity.
