Yucca

Yuccas are evergreen, perennial shrubs or trees with tough, sword-shaped leaves and large clusters of white, rounded to bell-shaped flowers. Several species are native to the Southeast and they are described here. A large clump of Spanish bayonet in early spring.

Best conditions

  • insolation : Bright, indirect sunlight
  • room temperature : 15 - 27 °C
  • room humidity : 30% - 50%
  • soil moisture : 50% - 70%
  • watering frequency : Every 10 days
Tips and Tricks

  • Intense, direct sunlight may cause white spots on leaves or crispy, brown tips.
  • Slightly cooler temperatures between 13-18Â°C during winter months will encourage healthy growth next spring.
  • Ensure that your plant is growing in soil-based compost with added grit for drainage.
