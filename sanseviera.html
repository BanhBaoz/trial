Sansevieria

Sansevierias are known for their tough, leathery leaves that have beautiful marbled patterns in greens, yellows, grays, and creams. They usually come as tall, stemless plants with sword-like leaves with sharp points or short rosettes. The foliage is typically green with marbled patterns and bands.

Best conditions

  • insolation : Bright, indirect sunlight, partial sun
  • room temperature : 18 - 24 °C
  • room humidity : 40% - 50%
  • soil moisture : 30% - 40%
  • watering frequency : Every 8-10 days during growing season and every 3-4 weeks during winter
Tips and Tricks

  • Only water the soil when it's completely dry.
  • Partial sun works best for snake plants, though it can still grow in darker corners or in brighter window areas.
  • Repot in spring, if needed, using houseplant compost.
