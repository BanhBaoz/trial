Ficus

Ficus are woody plants with highly varied forms: bushes, shrubs, small trees, and very tall and large trees. Roots are very extended, and leaves are usually simple, alternate, entire, or lobate. The plants may be evergreen, with large stipules surrounding the apical bud.

Best conditions
  • insolation : Bright, indirect sunlight
  • room temperature : 21 - 25 °C
  • room humidity : 50% - 75%
  • soil moisture : 40% - 60%
  • watering frequency : Every 5-7 days
Tips and Tricks

  • Ficus enjoy short bursts of direct sunlight, but do not to leave it in the sun for hours.
  • Sudden temperature drops may cause a leaf drop. Keep it away from cold drafts, such as near doors or drafty windows.
  • They prefer to dry out a bit in between watering, but do not let the soil to completely dry out.
