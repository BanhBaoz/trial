General Purpose

Our project: “Home plants monitoring and support” focuses on helping fellow plant parents keep their plants alive in best condition for as long as possible. Many people seem to struggle with proper care of their plants at home. Sometimes indoor/potted plants are treated as décor and with that they don’t receive enough care. Our mission is to increase awareness and knowledge about taking proper care of them. But mostly we want to provide users with a tool to monitor and help with plant maintenance. Our project focuses only on living indoor plants.

Basic Info

Our main concept is to create a working sensor which will help monitor and inform plant owners in real time about their plant’s environment. We will focus only on the types collected from the poll and do thorough research on them. Based on the data we will create a web page with gathered information about taking care of the plants. Later we plan on testing our sensors to see if they contribute to the increase of the plant's quality of life.
